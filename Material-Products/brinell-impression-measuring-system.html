Brinell Impression Measuring System (B.I.A.S)

Eliminates Operator Influence

The B.I.A.S virtually eliminates operator influence in Brinell hardness testing. It is found that manual measurement of Brinell indentations can lead to major variation between different operators. The B.I.A.S ensures consistent and accurate results every time.

This system intelligently removes human judgment errors by using optical technology that automatically detects and measures indentation diameter.
