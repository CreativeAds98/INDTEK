Universal Testing Machine (UTM)

Common Features & Applications

Universal Testing Machines support tension, compression, bend, re-bend, shear, pullout, Brinell, transverse and more. Supports flat belts, chain links, wire ropes, nut-bolts, round & irregular specimens.

Construction

The loading frame includes a central hydraulic cylinder and two main screws. A crosshead moves with chain transmission & geared motor. A precision piston-cylinder assembly ensures smooth force transfer.

Hydraulic System

Radial plunger pump delivers non-pulsating flow up to 210 bar. Includes filter, breather, indicator & precision flow control valve for infinitely variable piston speeds.

Electrical System

Separate electrical panel with interlocks preventing simultaneous motor operation. Limit switches protect against over-stroke.

Special Features

  • Front-open crossheads for effortless loading.
  • Optional hydraulic grips enable push-button clamping.
  • Specimen remains clamped after breaking.
  • Closed-loop PID control with advanced valves.
  • USB DAS connectivity with real-time graphing.

Principle of Operation

  • Oil pressure converted to electrical load signal via transducer.
  • Crosshead displacement measured using optical rotary encoder.
  • Motors interlocked for complete safety.
  • Dust-proof high-precision sensors and encoders.
