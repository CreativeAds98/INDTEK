Description

Brinell Hardness Tester OPAB-3000-IPC is designed for Brinell hardness measurement on steels, ferrous materials, and non-ferrous materials such as aluminium, bronze, and brass. Suited for cast, forged, rolled, or irregular-shaped components.

Main Features

  • Dead-weight load system with mechanical lever arrangement.
  • Integrated hydraulic power pack for smooth and stable operation.
  • Unique floating fulcrum ensures high accuracy.
  • Automatic indenter indexing for quick CCD-based measurement.
  • Reduces cycle time and improves operator efficiency.
  • Ideal for production labs, foundries, forging & heat treatment units.
  • Complies with IS 2281:2005 & BS-240.

Special Additional Features

  • Fully computerized indentation measurement and display.
  • High-quality CCD camera with 60X magnification.
  • Auto / Semi-Auto / Manual test options.
  • GO / NO-GO quick comparison mode.
  • 5-point calibration supported (Axial & Radial).
  • Batch testing, statistical analysis, certificate generation.

BrinSoft Software Features

  • Advanced image processing for precise impression measurement.
  • Auto, Semi-Auto & Compare modes for different finish conditions.
  • Pixel, multi-point & ERMA calibration.
  • Searchable certificates & historical test records.
  • Graphical statistics: Histogram, Waterfall, Frequency distribution.
