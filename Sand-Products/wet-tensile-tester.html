Test

Determines the wet tensile strength of foundry sand.

Application

When clay-bonded sand mould surfaces are strongly heated for a defined duration, part of the water evaporates and recondenses in the cooler sand region, causing a significant drop in strength in that condensation zone.

The Wet Tensile Tester measures this tensile strength drop in the condensation zone, helping determine mould behavior under thermal load.
